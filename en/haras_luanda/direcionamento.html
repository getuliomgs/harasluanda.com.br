The animal selection on Haras Luanda is motivated by the goal of producing complete, higher qualities on breed characterization animals, at the same time as we look for genetic character progress. In a continuous effort to achieve this goal, we structured our selection work over a consistent genetic basis, conferred by animals that gather all these characteristics.

After many investments and many kinds of research in horse’s bloodline, we defined our work over 04 animals that gather all those referred qualities and more, animals able to transfer its character to its descendants. They are the stallions Iluminado de Alfenas and OP de Santa Rita and the broodmares Viação de Sans Souci and Pintura de Santa Rita.
